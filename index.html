about

I'm studying Electrical Engineering at Caltech with a focus on Circuits and Electronics. My work tends to land somewhere between hardware and neuroscience, and I aim to continue diving into the world of neural interfaces and BCIs through mixed-signal IC design.

Outside of research and coursework, I run for the Caltech cross country and track teams at the NCAA Division III level, am a violinist for the Caltech Symphony Orchestra, and enjoy tutoring students through CalTutors and through my own private tutoring.
