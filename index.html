about

I'm studying Electrical Engineering at Caltech with a focus on Circuits and Electronics. My work tends to land somewhere between hardware and biology — I'm drawn to problems where you need to understand both the physics of a circuit and the system it's trying to interact with.

Outside of research and coursework, I run cross country and track for Caltech's Division III team, play violin in the Symphony Orchestra, and tutor students through CalTutors.
